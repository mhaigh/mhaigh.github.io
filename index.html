Research

Shallow-water double-gyre regime

A coherent meandering eastward jet is relatively simple to simulate using the quasi-geostrophic model, but surprisingly difficult using the shallow-water model. Part of my research involves attempting to attain a realistic double-gyre regime in the shallow-water model, and understanding the role of fluxes in the system.
